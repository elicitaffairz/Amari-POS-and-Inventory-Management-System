Overview
About This Manual

This manual guides you through the Amari POS & Inventory Management System — a role-based platform designed for efficient point-of-sale transactions and real-time inventory tracking. Each section follows a structured approach to help you understand not just how to use the system, but why each interaction works the way it does.

System Modules
ModulePurpose
DashboardReal-time overview of today's sales, transactions, and low stock alerts.
POSProcess customer transactions with cart, payment, and receipt generation.
InventoryManage products and ingredients — add, edit, delete, and monitor stock.
ReportsView sales summaries, charts, and export transaction history.
SettingsConfigure users, VAT rate, receipt info, and theme preferences.
User Roles & Access
RoleAccess Level
OwnerFull access — all modules including user management.
ManagerDashboard, POS, Inventory, Reports. No user management.
CashierDashboard (limited) and POS only. No Inventory or Reports.
💡
Before you begin Ensure you have received your login credentials from the system administrator. Each role has specific permissions designed to match job responsibilities.

Troubleshooting & FAQs

If something is not working as expected, check below. For issues not listed here, contact your system administrator.

Q
I cannot log in. What should I do?
Check that you are using the correct username and password — both fields are case-sensitive and Caps Lock must be off. If you have forgotten your password, ask your Owner or Manager to reset it under Settings → Users.
Q
Stock levels look wrong after a sale.
Stock is deducted automatically when a sale is completed. Go to Inventory → Products, click ✏ Edit on the product, and correct the quantity. For recurring discrepancies, review the transaction history in Reports.
Q
The Reports page shows no data.
Check the date filter at the top of the Reports page. Reset it to the past 30 days. If the page is still empty, confirm that at least one sale has been completed in the system.
Q
The barcode scanner is not working.
Check that your browser has camera permission enabled — look for the camera icon in the address bar and click Allow. Refresh the POS page after granting access. If the issue continues, search for the product by name instead.
