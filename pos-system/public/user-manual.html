Overview
About This System

Amari POS & Inventory Management System is a secure, role-based platform for point-of-sale operations and inventory management. Each staff member logs in with a personal username and password. Access is controlled by role — what you can see and do depends on whether you are an Owner, Manager, or Cashier.

Inventory Management

The Inventory module is where you maintain your sellable products and raw ingredients. Use it daily to keep pricing accurate, avoid stockouts, and ensure every POS sale deducts the correct items. Access is limited to Owner and Manager roles.

What You Can Do in Inventory
  • Products — add new menu items, update prices, and manage availability.
  • Ingredients — track raw stock levels and minimum thresholds.
  • Stock control — identify low inventory early to prevent missed sales.

Troubleshooting & Common Questions

If something is not working as expected, check the relevant answer below. For issues not listed here, contact your system administrator.

Q
I cannot log in. What should I do?
Make sure you are using the correct email and password — both fields are case-sensitive. Check that Caps Lock is off. If you have forgotten your password, ask your Owner or Manager to reset it under Settings → Users.
Q
Stock levels look wrong after a sale.
Stock is deducted automatically when a sale is completed. Go to Inventory → Products, click Edit on the product, and correct the quantity manually. For recurring issues, review the transaction history in Reports.
Q
The Reports page shows no data.
Check the date filter at the top of the page. Reset it to the past 30 days to see if results appear. If the page is still empty, confirm that at least one sale has been completed in the system.
Q
The barcode scanner is not working.
Check that your browser has camera permission enabled — look for the camera icon in the address bar and click Allow. Refresh the POS page after granting access. If the issue continues, try a different browser or search for the product manually by name.
